- S23 #cursor-equality-bug-in-effect-drain: Both `drainAudioEffects` and `runGodlyMatcher` used `if (effect.tick <= cursor) continue` with cursor advancing to `world.tick` at end of frame. AUDIT all cursor patterns with `<=` against tick-driven event streams across the codebase: anywhere you have a cursor and same-tick events from external input, the check should be `<` not `<=`.
