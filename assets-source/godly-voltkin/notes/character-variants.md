# Voltkin — Character Silhouette Variants

**Phase:** 1 (pre-lock brainstorm)
**Source:** Gemini 2.5 Pro, 2026-05-13
**Status:** Five candidates. One will be locked in `character-locked.md`.

All variants satisfy the DESIGN_BRIEF §3 anti-checklist (no pointy mouse ears, no red cheeks, no zigzag tail, no slim-mouse silhouette, no red in palette).

---

## 1. Gourd Gadget

- **Silhouette:** Pear / bowling-pin vertical — bottom-heavy, small head, gourd-with-limbs.
- **Distinctive feature (option a):** Single antenna stub on crown, capped with large pulsing blue sphere.
- **Tail:** Coiled-cord telephone-cable curl.
- **Anti-Pikachu rationale:** Vertical teardrop, not horizontal mouse. Single central antenna instead of two pointy ears. Tail is a tech-prop, not biological.
- **Pikachu-confusion risk:** 2/10.

## 2. Battery Blob

- **Silhouette:** Squat, earless, head+body fused — pure floor-hugging potato.
- **Distinctive feature (option c):** Horizontal row of 3 glowing orange circles across belly (power-level indicator).
- **Tail:** None / vestigial nub.
- **Anti-Pikachu rationale:** Pure earless blob, fundamentally not a mouse. Belly-circles are tech-inspired, not organic cheek pouches.
- **Pikachu-confusion risk:** 1/10. (Safest variant.)

## 3. Static Gremlin

- **Silhouette:** Chunky square body, wide toothy grin, mischievous posture.
- **Distinctive feature (option b):** Crown of 5 short thick yellow spikes (punk haircut) with blue spark arcs between tips.
- **Ears:** Small round nubs on sides.
- **Tail:** Thick stubby club.
- **Anti-Pikachu rationale:** Aggressive blocky body, spikes on head (not stripes on back), club tail. Reads "90s Digimon" more than "Pokémon."
- **Pikachu-confusion risk:** 3/10.

## 4. Capacitor Critter

- **Silhouette:** Perfectly spherical ball body, stubby limbs plugged in like a living capacitor.
- **Distinctive feature (creative — combines a+c spirit):** Two metallic blue plug-shaped "ears" stamped + and − like electrical terminals.
- **Tail:** Smooth C-curl.
- **Anti-Pikachu rationale:** Geometric sphere, not organism. Mechanical plug-ears unmistakably inorganic.
- **Pikachu-confusion risk:** 1/10.

## 5. Plasma Pet

- **Silhouette:** Soft beanbag blob, very short limbs, almost amorphous.
- **Distinctive feature (creative):** Single large cyclopean blue eye; semi-translucent yellow body with visible orange plasma tendrils inside.
- **Tail:** Wispy crackling energy tail.
- **Anti-Pikachu rationale:** Cyclops + amorphous body push it out of "electric mouse" entirely into "energy being."
- **Pikachu-confusion risk:** 2/10.

## Decision criteria (applied in `character-locked.md`)

1. **IP risk score** (Grok adversarial review)
2. **Iconic readability at 256px** (sprite must be unmistakable across 6 poses)
3. **Hype factor** ("first-glance WTF" — DESIGN_BRIEF §13)
4. **Distinctive-feature distinctiveness** (do we get a strong silhouette signature?)
5. **Cinematic-translatability** (does it leap convincingly out of a CRT?)
